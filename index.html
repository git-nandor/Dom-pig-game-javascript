GAME RULES:
Players rolls a dice as many times as they whishes. Each result get added to the recent score. If the player rolls a 1, all his recent score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn. If the player rolls a double 6, all his scores gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn. The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his recent score gets added to his global score. After that, it's the next player's turn. The first player who reach the score limit on global score wins the game.
